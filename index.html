Harlem Coding Kids





At Harlem Coding Kids, children from ages 9-12 can learn to code for free!


It’s a 10-week group class of 3-5 children that meets once a week. The class is conducted virtually—so you can learn from anywhere! At Harlem Coding Kids, your children can learn to code while making friends and having a fun, challenging, and productive experience.

WHY CODING?


  • By learning to code, your children will learn to see and understand the world in a way that they wouldn’t otherwise! They will learn to break down problems into smaller parts, and think logically to come up with solutions.

  • In addition to improving your resume and college application, learning to code is an enjoyable experience when taught in a fun and engaging way as we aim to do.

  • Technology is the future. According to Code.com, 71% of new jobs in STEM are in computer science, but only 25% of schools teach it. That's where we come in.

  • At Harlem Coding Kids, our goal is to teach computer science to children who don't have access to it. Coding contributes to almost all aspects of modern life, your cell phone, tv, cars, and even traffic lights.


  • By learning to code now, your children will not only get a head start in their stem education, but a head start in their careers and their life.

    • WHY PYTHON?


    • Coding takes place in different “languages,” such as Python, Java, and C++. This class will focus on the basics of Python, one of the most commonly used and easily readable coding languages.

    • Python allows you to do many things. It can be used to create programs such as simple games and translators for different languages, study tools, or secret codes creator and decoder for private communication.

    • It can also be used to analyze data. Eventually, you can complete complex mathematical functions and create more sophisticated programs. It is helpful in many fields, from finance to medicine.

    • After mastering Python, you can go on to learn other coding languages with a solid foundation.

    • Python is widely considered the best language with which to begin your coding journey.
