Lesson 4 - Data Collection Part I - In this lesson, we will learn how to hold and collect data in lists.

Lesson 5 - Conditionals -
Conditionals are statements that tell a computer what to do in specific conditions. A real-life example is; if it is raining, bring an umbrella, or else, do not bring an umbrella. This week, we will learn how to tell a computer how to do this.

Lesson 6 - Loops- This week, we will do a review of conditionals, and begin learning about for loops. A for loop tells a computer to perform a certain task a certain amount of times.

Lesson 7 - Review - This lesson will be a review of what we have already learned, specifically conditionals and for loops.

8 - Data Collection Part II - Dictionaries are a more advanced way to hold and collect data. We will learn how to create and use dictionaries, as well as how to manipulate them to optimize for our needs.

Lesson 9 - Functions - We will learn how to create our very own functions much like the ones we have been using. We write a function that uses everything that we have learned.

Lesson 10 - The End - Time for any lingering question and to finish up our functions.
